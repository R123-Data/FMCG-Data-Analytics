Title : FMCG Data Analytics

Description: This project is an interactive FMCG Sales & Business Intelligence Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, product trends, regional performance, payment behavior, and business growth across different FMCG categories and brands.The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

Tools & Technologies Used: 
Power BI - Main Data Visualization Platform used for report creation.
Power Query - Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) - Used for calcuated measures,dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.
Data Modeling - Relationships established among tables to enable cross-filteringand aggregation.
Interactive Visualizations 
KPI Cards & Slicers 

Objectives:
Analyze overall sales, profit, and quantity sold.
Identify top-performing brands, categories, and products.
Monitor monthly, quarterly, and regional sales trends.
Evaluate payment method preferences of customers.
Compare sales performance across states, regions, and business owners.
Provide interactive filtering for deeper business analysis.

Key KPIs:
Total Sales Revenue: ₹273.47K
Total Profit: ₹63.83K
Total Quantity Sold: 2,516 Units

Dashboard Insights:
1. Sales Performance Analysis -
Dairy and Beverages emerged as the highest revenue-generating categories.
Strong sales growth observed during Q4, contributing significantly to annual revenue.
November and December recorded peak monthly sales performance.
2. Brand & Product Analysis -
Amul generated the highest sales among all brands.
Butter, Tea, and Detergent were the top-selling sub-categories.
Product-level filters allow detailed performance evaluation.
3. Regional & Geographic Analysis -
Western region recorded the highest sales contribution.
State-wise analysis helps identify high-performing markets.
Interactive map visualization provides geographical sales distribution.
4. Customer Payment Behavior -
UPI, Card, and Cash transactions contributed almost equally to total sales.
Digital payment methods accounted for a major share of customer purchases.
5. Profitability Analysis -
Sales representatives were ranked based on profit contribution.
Profit trends help identify high-performing sales personnel and business owners.

Business Impact
This dashboard enables FMCG businesses to:
Track revenue and profitability efficiently.
Identify top-performing products and categories.
Understand customer purchasing patterns.
Monitor regional sales performance.
Support strategic decision-making through data-driven insights.
